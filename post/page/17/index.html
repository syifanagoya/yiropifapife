Imbolc, also referred to as Candlemas or Oimelc, occurs on February 2, 2022. This is an exciting holiday of the returning light. Spring is coming! The Celtic holiday honors Brigid, the fire goddess of healing, inspiration, and smithcraft. The mantra is refresh, renew, rededicate!
