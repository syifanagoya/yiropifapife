Early Life & Biography

She was born on 11th June in 1945 in Sacramento in California. She is the daughter of Joseph Barbeau and Armene Barbeau. Her mother was a housewife while her father was a public relations executive for MOBIL OIL. Her mother belonged to Armenian descent while her father belonged to German, Irish and Canadian descent. She has her half-brother named Robert and a sister named Jocelyn. She used to go to DEL MAR HIGH SCHOOL in San Jose in California.
