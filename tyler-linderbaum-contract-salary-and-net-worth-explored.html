Tyler Linderbaum contract, salary and net worth explored

Tyler Linderbaum, a center for the National Football League’s Baltimore Ravens, was born on April 7, 2000, in Solon, Iowa.

Linderbaum, who is of German ancestry later enrolled at Solon High School, where he participated in both offensive and defensive line play. In the 2018 U.S. Army All-American Game, Linderbaum participated. He made a commitment to play college football at the University of Iowa.

Prior to the 2019 season, Linderbaum, a defensive lineman who started his career at Iowa, switched to center. That season, he started all 13 of his games. In 2020, he started again and was recognized as an All-American and a Rimington Trophy finalist.

Tyler Linderbaum contract

Tyler signed a four-year, $13.2 million contract with the Ravens. That’s an average annual salary of $3.3 million. His contract has a $6.8 million signing bonus, and the $13.2 million is all guaranteed.

Tyler Linderbaum Salary

According to the contract, Tyler will earn a base salary of $705,000, with a signing bonus of $1.7 million. He has a cap hit of $2.4 million and dead cap value of $13.2 million.
