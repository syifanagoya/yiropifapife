Scott Wolf, Wife Kelley Welcome Baby Girl Lucy Marie: First Photo

Party of five! Scott Wolf and his wife Kelley welcomed their third child on Saturday, May 24, the actor’s rep confirms to Us Weekly.

The couple’s baby girl Lucy Marie arrived at 8:48 a.m., and weighed in at 6 lbs. and 13 oz. In an exclusive black-and-white photo of little Lucy (taken by her famous dad!) the proud parents showed off her adorable little foot for the first time.
