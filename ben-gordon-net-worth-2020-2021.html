Career

Ben Gordon started his NBA career when he was initially drafted for Chicago Bulls in the 2004 NBA Draft. In 2009, he signed a five-year deal with the Detroit Pistons, which cost $55 million. He consisted of his role as a sixth man. He scored the ten-millionth point in NBA history playing for Detroit. Later, he played for other teams such as Charlotte Bobcats (2012–2014), Orlando Magic (2014–2015), and Golden State Warriors (2015). His move to Orlando Magic was worth $9 million. During the end of his professional career, he played in the NBA Development League for Texas Legends.
