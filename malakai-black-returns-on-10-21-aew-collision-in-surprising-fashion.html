Malakai Black Returns On 10/21 AEW Collision In Surprising Fashion

AEW has a lot of creative irons in the fire, and a few stars are waiting for their comeback. This week, Malakai Black showed up in surprising fashion.

Ringside News has complete play-by-play coverage of AEW Collision. Nobody knew what would happen this week on Tony Khan’s Saturday show, that’s for sure.

Malakai Black made a significant return during this week’s AEW Collision on TNT. The event took place at the FedEx Forum in Memphis, TN, and marked the first appearance of The House of Black in quite some time.

The return of Malakai Black happened following the thrilling “Dream Match” opener, where Bryan Danielson defeated Andrade El Idolo in an outstanding contest.

After the match concluded, the arena’s lights went out, creating an air of suspense. When the lights came back on, Malakai Black delivered a devastating roundhouse kick that left The American Dragon, Bryan Danielson, down for the count. You can view video footage of Malakai Black’s dramatic return to AEW during this week’s Collision below.

We will have to see what’s next in this story. It appears that Bryan Danielson has another ex WWE Superstar to face next in Malakai Black. If anything, the BCC vs House of Black could be a compelling war.
